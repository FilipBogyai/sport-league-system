Player Guide

Once again welcome. We are exceptionally pleased that you decided to use this portal and registered here in order to create your player profile. On this particular page you'll find everything you'll need to jumpstart your glamouring sport career.

Please follow the steps in the following section to start playing as soon as possible.

What to do
  • In order to start playing as soon as possible please click on Sports tab and pick your favourite sports.
  • Navigate to each sport's leagues, pick those you deem worthy of your skills and click sign in button.
  • Now all the leagues you have signed in are present under My Leagues tab.
  • Once other players add themselves into some of your chosen leagues and league supervisor generates matches for you, you can enter My Matches to view them. Here you will find anything you need to know about your rivals and you can contact them in order to schedule a meeting.
  • After match has been played you can enter its outcome in the respective fields. After your rival does the same it is up to league supervisor to accept these data. Your points will be given to you shortly after being done so.
